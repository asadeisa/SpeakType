## Agent roles & delegation

How the team is organized and the delegation policy live in a separate file:

**@delegation.md**

### When to read it

Read `@doc/delegation.md` at the **start of any task** when:

- You are deciding whether to do the work yourself or delegate it.
- You are spawning or coordinating other agents (Sonnet, Haiku, or the team).
- You need to know the leader/orchestrator rules for this project.

Quick gist (full details in that file): **Opus 4.8 is the leader** and
**auto-delegates** long or repetitive execution to faster models, while keeping
planning and review for itself.
